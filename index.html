Project Description

  The project aims to visually represent migration trends in the United States from 2018 to 2022, including the impact of COVID-19. It seeks to provide insights into state-specific immigration, and changes in immigrant demographics over five years. By addressing these questions, the project aims to fill information gaps, offer perspectives on migration factors, and inspire inclusive policies acknowledging immigrants' contributions to American culture.
